Espresso

What Is Espresso Brewing

Espresso brewing is a method of coffee preparation that produces a concentrated and full-bodied beverage by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee under high pressure. Traditional espresso brewing focuses on precise timing and technique to create a rich flavor, strong aroma, and a distinctive layer of crema.

Variety of Brewing Methods

Modern espresso brewing builds on this foundation by using advanced machines that offer accurate control over temperature and pressure. This allows for greater consistency and refinement, enabling baristas to achieve balanced extractions while preserving the intensity and character of the coffee.
